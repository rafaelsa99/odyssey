Odyssey - {% trans "Platform for Automated Sensing in Archaeology" %}

 

"Odyssey" is a Portuguese research project that merges the potential of geoinformatics and the study of the past. Formed by a multidisciplinary team of archaeologists and computer engineers, the project aims to locate archaeological sites by employing non-invasive remote-sensing techniques. This approach is relatively new in archaeological research and the first of its kind in Portuguese territory.

The project takes place from 2021 to 2023 and it is funded by the European Union
